My Strongest Trait: Perseverance

My strongest trait is perseverance and it has greatly impacted my academic life and my way of thinking. Whether it's taking on challenging assignments, getting ready for tests or efficiently managing my time, I'm dedicated to conquering any obstacles that arise in my academic life. This trait has helped me maintain high grades, complete assignments on time, stay focused even when things get tough and most importantly never give up. Playing video games and reading are two of my favorite hobbies that help's me to stay focused. Taking a quick break from a tedious assignment keeps my mind clear and ready to tackle any assignment I'm stuck on.

Favorite Quote: "The only way to do great work is to love what you do." - Steve Jobs

This quote resonates alot with me because of not only the quote but also the time and place it was said. Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple, was giving a speach at Stanford Univerity where he shared his life story and the challeges he faced in his life. The quote speaks to loving what you do and not to do what you don't like because it will lead to your downfall. When you love what you do nothing can stop you from pushing the boundaries limiting creativity and innovation. I strive to apply this mindset not only to my academic life but also other aspects of my life. Rest in peace my hero, I hope i will be able to follow in your foot steps and create something great for the world to enjoy.
